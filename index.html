AMA University: A Legacy of Academic Excellence

AMA University, founded in 1980 by Dr. Amable R. Aguiluz V, is a renowned institution in the Philippines. It offers diverse programs ranging from business to engineering. What sets AMA apart is its focus on technology and innovation, providing students with hands-on experience. The institution boasts a dedicated faculty and strong industry partnerships, ensuring students are well-prepared for their careers. Beyond academics, AMA emphasizes character development and holistic growth.

AMA University Goes Global

AMA University's global presence is expanding with international campuses. AMA International University-Bahrain and AMA International University-Dubai are shining examples. They offer quality education in various fields and have become respected institutions in the Middle East. These international branches have facilitated cultural exchange and global collaboration, making AMA a true global educational powerhouse.

AMA University's Vision for Tomorrow's Education

With a legacy steeped in academic prowess, AMA University continues to innovate for the future. Its tech-centric curriculum evolves in step with advancing technology, ensuring graduates remain ahead in their careers. AMA's international campuses represent its global vision, promoting cross-cultural ties and a diverse learning environment. Through a blend of academic rigor, industry collaboration, and global outreach, AMA University paves the way for a new era of education, preparing students to thrive in an ever-evolving world.
